A WonderLeap STEM & Future Tech Labs Adventure

What your child has learned

  • Engineers use science, maths and creative thinking to solve problems and build things that help people.
  • They build bridges, houses, vehicles, robots and lots of useful machines.
  • Engineers are clever, creative, work in teams, and don't give up.

Questions to ask afterwards

  • What's something an engineer might build for our town?
  • If you were an engineer, what tricky problem would you solve?
  • Can you draw an invention for me?

Try this together

  • Build a bridge from blocks, books or LEGO and walk a toy car across.
  • Make a den or simple shelter from sofa cushions — talk about "what holds it up?".
  • Take apart an old broken toy together (safely) to peek inside.
  • Visit a building site or look at a bridge on a walk — point out the structures.
